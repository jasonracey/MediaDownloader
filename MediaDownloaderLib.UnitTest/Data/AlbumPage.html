Psychotic Reaction Norman, Oklahoma

High Energy Rock and Roll band from Norman, OK. est. 2007 heavily influenced by The Stooges, MC5, Radio Birdman, 13th Floor Elevators, The Yardbirds, Black Sabbath, Pink Floyd, early country blues music, H.P. Lovecraft, George Orwell and Lucid Dreams .
